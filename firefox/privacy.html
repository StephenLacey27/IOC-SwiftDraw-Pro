Privacy Policy for IOC SwiftDraw Pro

Last updated: 2025-05-25

1. Introduction

IOC SwiftDraw Pro (“the Extension”) is a Firefox browser extension that lets you right-click on Indicators of Compromise (IP, hash, URL, etc.) and launch them in your chosen OSINT tools. Your privacy is important—this policy explains what (if any) data we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

  • User selections: When you highlight or right-click a string, we read that selection only to construct the context-menu entry and open the corresponding URL.
  • Extension settings: We store which OSINT sources you’ve enabled or disabled via Firefox’s browser.storage API.

We do not collect any browsing history beyond your immediate right-click selection, nor do we collect personal identifiers, IP addresses, or other usage analytics.

3. How We Use Information

All data is used solely to:

  1. Generate context-menu entries for the indicator you select.
  2. Remember your source-selection preferences between browser sessions.

4. Data Sharing

We never share, sell, or transmit any data you generate with the Extension to third parties. All operations run entirely within your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not send usage data to external analytics platforms. When you launch an OSINT tool link, that domain’s own privacy policy applies, but IOC SwiftDraw Pro does not proxy or log those requests.

6. Security

We use Firefox's built-in storage sandbox to store your preferences. No remote servers are involved, and no data leaves your machine except when you explicitly launch a third-party tool in a new tab.

7. Children’s Privacy

This Extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
